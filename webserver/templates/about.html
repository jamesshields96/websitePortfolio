Me

Hi, I'm James and I am a 23 year old student at Monash University studying a Bachelors of Information Technology, majoring in software development. I am currently looking for any internships in software engineering field which can help supplement my learning and help me grow as a person.

Hobbies

I try and get up to the mountains as much as possible but due to Australia's small snow season and generally poor condidtions it can sometimes be hard to make it up. In my spare time, I enjoy reseraching new topics and undertaking a variety of projects to help me learn about new topics in the field of computer science.
